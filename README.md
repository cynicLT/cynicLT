## 👋 About

Senior/Lead Java Engineer and **Architect** with **17+ years** designing and delivering high-load fintech and enterprise systems. I specialise in **Java/Spring microservices, AWS cloud migrations, and Kubernetes-based platforms** — and I lead the engineering teams that build them.

Over six years at **Zenitech** I progressed from Senior Engineer → **Technical Lead** → **Principal Engineer** → **Java Chapter Lead**, owning distributed-system architecture and mentoring engineers across multiple teams. I've contributed to **Allianz Technology's** digitalisation programme and currently architect solutions on **Rivile's GAMA platform**, used by roughly **one in three Lithuanian companies**.

**What I bring**
- 🏗️ Deep technical ownership of mission-critical, high-availability systems
- 🧩 The ability to turn ambiguous business problems into clean, maintainable architecture
- 🌱 A track record of growing engineers into stronger contributors and raising the technical bar

> Open to **Engineering Manager / CTO**, **Principal**, and **Senior/Lead Engineer & Architect** roles — fintech, cloud-native SaaS, or scale-up environments.
